Tokuro Fujiwara is a Japanese video game designer, active from about 1982 to 2009. He was involved in many formative Capcom titles as a director or producer.

Essential works:
- [[Pooyan]]
- [[Roc'n Rope]]
- *Ghosts 'n Goblins*
- *Bionic Commando*
- *Sweet Home*
- *Mega Man 2* (Producer)
